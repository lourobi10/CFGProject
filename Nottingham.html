Nottingham

Nottingham is a city located in the East Midlands of England. It's found 260 kilometres north of London. It is recognised as the sixth biggest economy in the United Kingdom, with an estimated population of about 330,000 people in 2020. It's also well-known for it's ties to the legend of Robin Hood.
